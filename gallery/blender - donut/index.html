Blender Donut Project

If you have ever researched the free 3D software Blender, you’ve likely come across many renders of delicious-looking donuts. This is because the most popular tutorial series for getting started with Blender is the free Donut Course by Blender Guru. As a complete novice to Blender, I decided to give the course a try, and share what I learned. Hopefully, this will give you some insight if you’re thinking about taking the course, too!
